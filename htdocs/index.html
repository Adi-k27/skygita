940, 941 Payroll Trust Fund Accounts

Trust fund tax liabilities, assessed under IRC 6672, cause many businesses to fail with a red IRS seizure sticker attached to the businesses’ doors. Trust fund liabilities are easily transferred to the business’s owners and key employees. Often these taxes are satisfied by the filing of liens, levies, judgements and the seizing of bank accounts, cars and boats belonging to the business’s principals as well as business assets. None of these actions require the IRS to go to court. The penalties are not dischargeable in bankruptcy. At ASKi, we have a retired IRS Revenue Officer, with many years of experience in the untangling of these problematic accounts, and the obtaining of relief from the numerous types of penalties associated with them.

Skygita. Awarded $5 Million BPA by US Department of Transportation Federal Transit Administration

Alexandria, VA (October 22, 2009) – Skygita. announced today that it has been selected by the US Department of Transportation (DOT) to receive a Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) at the Federal Transportation Administration (FTA).

The BPA is intended to support FTA’s Office of Budget and Policy technical and program support initiatives including the coordination of its finance, accounting, and budget program activities. These services will support FTA’s mission and the volume of purchases issued under this BPA has a $5 million ceiling value over the five year period of performance.

This contract was awarded with confidence in Aski’s expertise with Federal financial policies, procedures and regulation as well as the firm’s past performance with Federal agencies, local and state government and commercial clients.

"In my opinion, Aski’s competitive proposal and commitment to the customer over the last 12 years made all the difference in us winning this contract," said Indira Kumar, Aski Vice President. "Under this contract, we will expand upon the services that we provide to Federal agencies and combine resources with teaming partners to supply the ultimate in best value to the Federal government."

“It’s been a tremendous year at Aski, with key wins that reach across our entire organization,” added Mrs. Kumar. “Our employees and partners have continued to rally together to deliver unparalleled support and services to our customers, and our customers have noticed. We look forward to working with DOT stakeholders. With our combined resources, we will be able develop and refine the tools and information needed to support FTA finance, budget, and accounting operations.”
